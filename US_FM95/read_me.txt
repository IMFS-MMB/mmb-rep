US_FM95 Replication

+ last change: 2008-12-08

+ replication: IRF to monetary shock (unit shock); figure 2 in Levin et al. (2003)

+ file with the comparisson of results: US_FM95_rep_results.xls 

+ file to produce original results: fmcall_RUNNING.m in folder labeled US_FM95_orig
  IRF and ACF (COR) can then be found in the workspace 

+ file to produce replicated IRF: run.m (which executes US_FM95_rep.mod in the folder labeled US_FM95_rep)


+ software requirement: DYNARE version 4

+ literature:
  - Fuhrer and Moore, Inflation Persistence, Quaterly Journal of Economics, 1995, 110 (1)
  - Fuhrer and Moore, Monetary Policy Trade-offs and the Correlation between Nominal Interest Rates and Real            Output, American Economic Review, 1995, 85 (1)
  - Levin, Wieland and Williams, The Performance of Forecast-Based Monetary Policy Ruled under Model Uncertainty,
    American Economic Review, 2003, 93 (3)
